02 — Training mechanics

Intelligence is in the torque.

A conventional system gives you fixed, friction-based load. QDD lets the motor apply an exact, programmed force — and change it instantly between resistance and assistance.

Resisted sprint & acceleration

Apply controlled load to build force and drive-phase mechanics, then release it to test transfer to free running.

Assisted overspeed

Precisely-controlled acceleration pulls athletes to surpass peak speeds of up to 14 m/s — safely and progressively, never yanked.

Strength, eccentric & tempo

On the Strength Platform: squats, deadlifts and upper-body work with controlled eccentric and tempo profiles.
